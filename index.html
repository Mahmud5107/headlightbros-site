How it works
Simple 4-step process for every car.
  1. 1 We inspect your headlights and tape off surrounding paint.
  2. 2 Wet-sand the lens to remove oxidation and old, damaged coating.
  3. 3 Machine polish the lens to restore clarity and shine.
  4. 4 Apply a UV protective coating so your headlights stay clear.
